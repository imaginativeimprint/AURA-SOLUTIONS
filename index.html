Atmos-Dome Home

An intelligent indoor air purifier and humidifier designed as premium home furnishing that builds a micro-climate for comfort and health.

Why Do We Need It?

Indoor air is often 2–5× more polluted than outdoor air. The Atmos-Dome reduces VOCs, allergens, viruses, and dust to improve respiratory health for family members.

Technology Used

Tri-Stage Filtration System with PCO (UV-based VOC breakdown), electrostatic particle capture, activated carbon deodorization, IoT smart sensors, and AI environment control.

High-Efficiency

Pathogen + VOC removal with low maintenance - no disposable HEPA filters needed.

AI Powered

Predicts pollution patterns and optimizes power use for maximum efficiency.

Optimal Humidity

Maintains 40-60% humidity with demineralized mist to prevent white dust.

Technical Design

Explore the innovative engineering behind the Atmos-Dome Home

Advanced Filtration System

The Atmos-Dome Home features a sophisticated tri-stage filtration system that removes particles as small as 0.1 microns, including viruses, bacteria, and volatile organic compounds.

Our proprietary PCO (Photocatalytic Oxidation) technology breaks down harmful VOCs at a molecular level, while the electrostatic particle capture system eliminates airborne contaminants without the need for disposable filters.

The integrated humidification system uses demineralized water to maintain optimal humidity levels between 40-60%, creating the perfect environment for respiratory health.

Atmos-Spire Urban Tower

Public air purification and micro-climate cooling infrastructure that protects pedestrians from pollution exposure and acts as a smart-city environmental node.

Why Do We Need It?

Reduces traffic-based PM2.5, PM10, NOx, fights urban heat island effect, and improves street-level air where humans breathe most.

Technology Used

Industrial-grade 3-Stage Filtration with PCO reactor, electrostatic PM capture, activated carbon pollutant removal, solar power, rainwater harvesting for misting, and 24/7 IoT monitoring.

Solar Powered

Can run off-grid with sustainable energy, reducing municipal energy costs.

Smart City Hub

Emergency SOS, speakers, Wi-Fi hotspot, and environmental data collection.

Eco-Friendly

Rainwater harvesting for misting system and sustainable material construction.

Urban Infrastructure Design

Discover the engineering excellence of the Atmos-Spire Urban Tower

Smart City Integration

The Atmos-Spire Urban Tower is designed as a multifunctional urban infrastructure element that serves as both an air purification system and a smart city node.

Its industrial-grade filtration system can process up to 10,000 cubic meters of air per hour, effectively cleaning the air in a radius of 50 meters around the tower.

The integrated rainwater harvesting system collects and filters water for the misting function, which helps cool the surrounding area and suppress dust particles.

Community Impact

Transforming urban spaces with cleaner air and enhanced public amenities

Enhanced Public Spaces

Urban residents enjoy cleaner air in parks, plazas, and pedestrian zones with Atmos-Spire installations.

Community Health Benefits

Reduced respiratory issues and improved quality of life for urban populations living in high-pollution areas.
